# SPINYL 🎧

SPINYL is a centralized music review and discovery web application designed for music enthusiasts who want to track, review, and discuss their favorite albums, tracks, and artists. Moving beyond simple streaming, SPINYL focuses on the culture of music criticism and community curation.

## 🚀 Features

* **Centralized Music Reviews:** Write, edit, and share comprehensive reviews for albums, EPs, and singles.
* **Dynamic Music Charts:** Stay updated with trending music and community-driven charts.
* **Deep Discovery:** Explore new artists, genres, and hidden gems through structured metadata.
* **User Profiles & Collection:** Keep a digital log of your listening history, favorite records, and personal ratings.
* **Seamless Integration:** Designed to pull rich music metadata to keep the platform accurate and up-to-date.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** React / Next.js (Optimized for fast rendering and SEO)
* **Backend & Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL, Realtime, and Authentication)
* **State Management & APIs:** Integrations with major music streaming developer dashboards for metadata synchronization.
* **Styling:** Modern, sleek UI with a high-contrast visual aesthetic.
